About Me

I briefly flirted with software development in college, but ultimately chose another path. Since then it has always been in the back of my mind, biding its time until recently when I decided to make it my future. I am thrilled to once again be exploring a field that I enjoy. I look forward to showing others what I can achieve through my hard work and my drive to excel and have high anticipation of working with a team of skilled developers learning what I can while also sharing what I can.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy being as active as I can be. Soccer has always been a passion of mine, so I play whenever I get the opportunity and watch it when I don't. Also, travel and the great outdoors have always called to me. I believe in exploring this great big world of ours and learning as much as we can about it.

I look forward to the opportunity to work with you providing any desktop/web application or mobile app service that you may need, so please feel free to contact me so that I may help you with any of your development needs!
